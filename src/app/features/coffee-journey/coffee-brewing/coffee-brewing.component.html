 Brewing Tips
  • Use freshly roasted beans, ideally within 2-4 weeks of the roast date
  • Grind your coffee just before brewing for maximum freshness
  • Use filtered water for the cleanest flavor
  • Maintain consistent water temperature throughout the brewing process
  • Invest in a quality burr grinder for consistent particle size
  • Measure your coffee and water with a scale for consistency
