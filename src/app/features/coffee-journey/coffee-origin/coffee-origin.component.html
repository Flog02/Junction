Understanding Coffee Origins

Terroir

Like wine, coffee is influenced by its terroir â€” the environmental factors like climate, soil, and elevation that affect the beans' taste.

Processing Methods

The way coffee cherries are processed after harvesting significantly impacts flavor. Common methods include washed, natural, and honey processing.

Varietals

Different coffee plant varieties (like Bourbon, Typica, or Gesha) have distinct flavor characteristics, similar to grape varieties in winemaking.

 Coffee Belt

Coffee grows in the "Bean Belt," the region between the Tropics of Cancer and Capricorn. This equatorial zone provides the ideal climate for coffee cultivation.
