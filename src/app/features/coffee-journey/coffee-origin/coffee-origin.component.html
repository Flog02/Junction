 Coffee Facts

Coffee Plant

Coffee is grown on trees that produce cherries. Each cherry typically contains two beans.

Coffee Belt

Coffee is grown in the "Bean Belt" - regions between the Tropics of Cancer and Capricorn.

Processing Methods

The most common processing methods are washed, natural, and honey, each creating different flavor profiles.

Elevation Matters

Coffee grown at higher elevations develops more complex flavors due to slower bean development.
