Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?

Yes, you need to be logged in to place a table order. This helps us track your order and provide a better experience.

How do I pay for my order?

You can pay securely through the app using a credit card, gift card, or loyalty points.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! You can customize your coffee just like you would at the counter.

What if I need to change my order?

You can modify your order before it's prepared. Once it's being prepared, please speak with our staff.
